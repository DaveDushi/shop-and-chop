#### 2. Recording Sequence (3 minutes)

**Scene 1: Hook (0:00-0:15)**
- Show messy meal planning (notepad, multiple tabs)
- Cut to clean Shop & Chop interface
- Narration: "Transform 3 hours of meal planning into 10 minutes"

**Scene 2: Recipe Browsing (0:15-0:45)**
- Navigate to Recipes page
- Show 100+ recipes
- Apply filters (vegetarian, gluten-free)
- Highlight search functionality
- Narration: "Browse curated recipes with smart filtering"

**Scene 3: Meal Planning (0:45-1:15)**
- Navigate to Meal Planner
- Drag "Chicken Stir Fry" to Tuesday dinner
- Drag "Pasta Salad" to Wednesday lunch
- Show quick week completion
- Narration: "Drag and drop meals onto your weekly calendar"

**Scene 4: Shopping List Generation (1:15-1:45)**
- Click "Generate Shopping List"
- Show instant generation (< 2 seconds)
- Highlight ingredient consolidation
- Show category organization
- Narration: "One click creates organized shopping lists"

**Scene 5: Mobile Experience (1:45-2:15)**
- Switch to mobile view (Chrome DevTools)
- Show offline functionality
- Demonstrate item checking
- Show category navigation
- Narration: "Mobile-optimized for in-store shopping"

**Scene 6: Technical Highlights (2:15-2:45)**
- Show PWA installation prompt
- Demonstrate offline mode
- Highlight TypeScript/React architecture
- Show performance metrics
- Narration: "Built with modern web technologies"

**Scene 7: Closing (2:45-3:00)**
- Show before/after time comparison
- Display key benefits
- Show GitHub/demo links
- Narration: "Shop smart, chop smarter with Shop & Chop"
