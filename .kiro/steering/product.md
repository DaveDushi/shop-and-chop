# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
A smart meal planner and shopping list generator that helps users plan their weekly meals and automatically generates organized shopping lists. The app eliminates the tedious task of manually creating shopping lists by analyzing selected meals and intelligently grouping ingredients by store category.

## Target Users
- **Busy professionals** who want to meal prep but lack time for planning
- **Families** looking to organize weekly grocery shopping and reduce food waste
- **Health-conscious individuals** who want structured meal planning
- **Budget-conscious shoppers** who want to optimize grocery spending
- **Anyone** who finds meal planning and grocery list creation tedious

## Key Features
- **Weekly Meal Planning**: Visual calendar interface for selecting meals
- **Recipe Database**: Curated collection of recipes with ingredients and instructions
- **Smart Shopping Lists**: Auto-generated lists organized by grocery store sections
- **Ingredient Consolidation**: Combines duplicate ingredients across multiple recipes
- **Dietary Preferences**: Filter recipes by dietary restrictions (vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.)
- **Portion Scaling**: Adjust recipe quantities based on household size
- **Shopping List Export**: Export lists to mobile-friendly formats
- **Meal History**: Track previously planned meals for easy re-selection

## Business Objectives
- **Primary Goal**: Create a seamless meal planning experience that saves users 2+ hours per week
- **User Engagement**: Achieve 70%+ weekly active usage among registered users
- **Problem Resolution**: Reduce food waste by 30% through better planning
- **Market Validation**: Demonstrate clear value proposition for potential monetization

## User Journey
1. **Onboarding**: Set dietary preferences, household size, and favorite cuisines
2. **Weekly Planning**: Browse recipes and drag-and-drop meals onto calendar
3. **List Generation**: Review auto-generated shopping list organized by store sections
4. **Shopping**: Use mobile-optimized list while grocery shopping
5. **Cooking**: Access recipes and cooking instructions throughout the week
6. **Feedback Loop**: Rate meals and build personalized recommendations

## Success Criteria
- **Time Savings**: Users report saving 2+ hours per week on meal planning
- **Adoption Rate**: 80%+ of users complete their first weekly meal plan
- **Retention**: 60%+ of users return to plan a second week
- **Accuracy**: Shopping lists have 95%+ ingredient accuracy
- **User Satisfaction**: Average rating of 4.5+ stars for ease of use
